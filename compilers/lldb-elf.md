### ELF and Executables in gneral
`lldb` is for `ELF` (Unix/Linux), `COFF` (Windows), and `Mach-O` (macOS)

### Side notes

Don't confuse `lld` and `lldb'
* ⁍ lld : LLVM linker 
* ⁍ lldb : LLVM debugger (similar to gdb)
